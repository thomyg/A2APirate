# The AgentCard

Your agent's **business card** for the A2A protocol.

---
layout: default
transition: slideRight
---

# What does an AgentCard tell the world?

- **Who am I?** — Name, description, version
- **What can I do?** — Skills, tags, capabilities
- **How do you reach me?** — Endpoint URL, protocol binding
- **Do I support streaming?** — Capabilities flag

No AgentCard = no A2A. Discovery is **not optional**.
